Project Overview

Reimagining the corporate
dinner as immersive experience

This bespoke closing dinner for Estée Lauder Companies transformed a raw airplane hangar into a sensorial journey of luxury and escape. From pitch to execution, I led the entire experience — curating a high-concept, opulent atmosphere through refined aesthetics, immersive details, and brand storytelling.

The challenge was environmental: a raw industrial space with no inherent luxury. The solution was total — every surface, every sightline, every sensory detail designed to make guests forget where they were and feel entirely transported.

01

Reimagine the corporate dinner as an immersive branded experience — something no one at ELC had seen before.

02

Seamlessly integrate themes of beauty, aviation, and luxury hospitality into one coherent visual world.

03

Maintain elegance and functionality within the significant environmental constraints of a raw industrial space.

04

Strengthen team unity and reaffirm ELC's commitment to elevated, branded experiences for its people.

01 · Concept & Moodboards

The vision before
the build

The concept began with a single question: what does it feel like to arrive somewhere extraordinary? The moodboards established the emotional register — opulent without being obvious, cinematic without being theatrical. Aviation as a metaphor for elevation, both literal and aspirational.

02 · Environment & Layout

Architecture of
the evening

The floor plan was designed as an emotional arc — arrival, immersion, peak, and close. Every zone was considered: the entry moment, the sightline from each seat, the lighting temperature as the evening progressed, the placement of florals to draw the eye toward the stage.

04 · Outcome & Reception

A new benchmark
for ELC

The event was met with exceptional praise and exceeded internal expectations across every measure. It set a new benchmark for experiential storytelling at Estée Lauder Companies — demonstrating that a corporate gathering could carry the same emotional weight as a luxury brand activation. The evening strengthened team unity and reaffirmed the brand's commitment to elevated, intentional experiences for its people.
